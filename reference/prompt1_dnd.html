KNAVE CAMPAIGN KICKOFF — SESSION –1 PROMPT PACK (2 pages, print landscape)

Goal: Player-authored ingredients + clear pressures + consequences + rewards + change over time. Rule: 1–2 sentences per answer.

TAB 1 — WHAT EXISTS

A) Quick prompts

  • 3 Truths everyone knows: 1) ____ 2) ____ 3) ____
  • 1 Everyday detail (food/custom/clothing): ____
  • 1 Big unknown everyone agrees is unknown: ____
  • 1 Non-negotiable fact shaping play (scarcity/danger/law/geo): ____

B) d12 Truth Category (3 columns)

1 Travel is dangerous because ____ 5 Authority is fragile/corrupt/absent: ____ 9 A ruin defines the region: ____
2 The dead don’t stay dead because ____ 6 A taboo people fear breaking: ____ 10 A local custom outsiders find weird: ____
3 Magic has a visible cost: ____ 7 A needed resource is scarce: ____ 11 A recent disaster still affects life: ____
4 The land is changing: ____ 8 A monster type is common here: ____ 12 A prophecy/omen people believe: ____

C) d12 Starting Situation (3 columns)

1 Road/bridge is out 5 Plague/sickness/curse rumored 9 Feud about to turn violent
2 Festival (something will go wrong) 6 Powerful stranger arrived yesterday 10 Monster migration underway
3 New cult/faith spreading 7 Something valuable found nearby 11 New law/restriction just changed
4 Tax/tribute overdue 8 People disappearing 12 Ancient seal/boundary broken

D) d8 Media Touchstone Extractor (2 columns)

1 Location vibe (feel/smell/sound) 5 Reveal (secret truth of the world)
2 Type of danger (predator/curse/politics) 6 Cool treasure (why it matters)
3 Moral dilemma (choose between ____ and ____) 7 Scene you want to experience
4 Signature NPC archetype (who + want) 8 Rule of the world (“Here, ____ always happens.”)

TAB 2 — WHAT WE WANT

Player desires (not character backstory). Creates pressure + direction.

A) Quick prompts

  • I light up when the game has: ____
  • I check out / fall asleep when the game has: ____
  • I want more: ____ / I want less: ____
  • I love when play rewards (pick 1): Cleverness / Courage / Planning / Improv / Teamwork / Risk
  • I do not care about: ____

B) d12 What I Want To Do (3 columns)

1 Outsmart a deadly situation 5 Build allies/crew/militia 9 Survive a chase/escape
2 Risky deal with a powerful NPC 6 Take down a faction from within 10 Command something (ship/keep/town/etc.)
3 Explore a place nobody returns from 7 Find a legendary treasure 11 Discover forbidden knowledge
4 Pull off a heist/con 8 Solve an unsettling mystery 12 Create lasting regional change

C) d12 I Hate When… (3 columns)

1 Endless combat, no stakes 5 Shopping drags 9 Too much talking, no doing
2 Vague goals/no direction 6 One-solution puzzles 10 Too much doing, no breath
3 Overplanning never pays 7 Random death, no warning 11 Powerless/railroaded
4 Lore dump monologues 8 Nothing has consequences 12 Tone whiplash

D) d6 Campaign Dial (pick 2 higher)

1 Mystery & weirdness 4 Dungeon horror/traps
2 Faction politics 5 Treasure & artifacts
3 Wilderness danger 6 Base-building & influence

TAB 3 — WHAT THREATENS

Problems/factions/dangers that act even if ignored.

A) Quick prompts

  • A danger already active: ____
  • A group with power who wants something: ____
  • A situation that will worsen if ignored: ____
  • A “soft” threat (social/economic/moral): ____

B) d12 Threat Type (3 columns)

1 Hungry/restless undead 5 Witch/curse/blight 9 Rival adventurers/mercs
2 Bandit confederation 6 Monster territory expands 10 Plague/madness/addiction
3 Cult/forbidden religion 7 Ancient ruin awakening 11 Border conflict/invasion
4 Noble house/law/oppression 8 Trade war/scarcity/famine 12 “Friendly” institution w/ dark core

C) d12 Faction Wants (3 columns)

1 Control resource (mine/water/food) 5 Secret buried in past 9 Stop/fulfill prophecy
2 Control route (road/river/pass) 6 Revenge (target) 10 Monopolize magic/knowledge
3 Relic/artifact 7 Legitimacy (titles/law) 11 “Purify” the region
4 A person (hostage/heir/prophet) 8 New recruits 12 Open/close gate or seal

D) d12 Doing This Right Now (3 columns)

1 Kidnapping/press-gang 5 Spread rumor to spark panic 9 Poison supply line
2 Extort/collect tribute 6 Sabotage rival 10 Raise/summon something
3 Burn evidence/silence 7 Excavate a ruin 11 Enforce new law violently
4 Corrupt officials/bribery 8 Recruit via “miracles” 12 Public example of someone

E) d8 If Ignored… Escalates (2 columns)

1 Spreads to new area5 Travel becomes riskier
2 Innocents hurt publicly6 Safe place becomes unsafe
3 Prices rise/scarcity7 Rival faction gains power
4 Key NPC flips sides8 Point-of-no-return event

TAB 4 — WHAT REWARDS

A) Quick prompts

  • I’ll risk my character’s life for: ____
  • I care about protecting: ____
  • If we found ____, I’d drop everything.
  • I would return to town if: ____ changed / became available.
  • Treasure should be: Useful / Weird / Dangerous / Political / Cursed

B) d12 Reward Types (3 columns)

1 Map to something worse 5 Specialist (sage/smith/healer) 9 Title/status
2 Tool that breaks limits 6 Base upgrade (walls/forge/etc.) 10 Crew/hirelings who stick
3 Favor owed by power 7 Secret that shifts alliances 11 Resource source (mine/herbs)
4 Safe route/shortcut 8 Magical oddity w/ side effects 12 Key to a locked place

C) d12 Town Pull (3 columns)

1 Only town has cure/priest 5 Ally needs help now 9 Weather forces regroup
2 Identify a relic 6 Wanted/hunted (lay low) 10 Faction demands meeting
3 Sell bulky loot 7 Festival/trial/election 11 New service opens (forge/market)
4 Need rumors/leads 8 Hire help for next trip 12 Someone followed you home

D) d8 Personal Fun Dial (2 columns; circle 2)

1 Outsmart trap/ambush5 Fight something truly scary
2 Gamble on risky plan6 Get rich → problems
3 Explore something eerie7 Change the town/world
4 Negotiate from weakness8 Uncover forbidden secret

TAB 5 — WHAT CHANGES OVER TIME

Use as evolution prompts. Roll 1–3 times.

A) d20 Thing That Can Change (4 columns)

1 Town population 6 Resource supply (food/iron/water) 11 Weather/season pattern 16 Curse/blight intensity
2 Town wealth 7 Local law/taboo 12 Price of essentials 17 Travel time/ease
3 Town safety 8 Who rules locally 13 Healing access 18 Border/boundary
4 Town reputation 9 Faction influence 14 Gear access (smith/arrows/oil) 19 Magic availability / fear
5 Trade route 10 Monster presence 15 Ruin access 20 Info/rumors circulation

B) d12 How It Changes (3 columns)

1 Grows/strengthens 5 Changes hands (new control) 9 Becomes public knowledge
2 Shrinks/weakens 6 Becomes contested (two claimants) 10 Becomes hidden/suppressed
3 Scarce/expensive 7 Corrupted/tainted 11 Collapses suddenly
4 Abundant/cheap 8 Purified/stabilized 12 Transforms into something else

C) d12 Why It Changes (3 columns)

1 Player action directly 5 Pact/deal made 9 Discovery (ruin/relic/vein/map)
2 Player inaction (ignored threat) 6 Betrayal 10 Death (leader/hero/villain)
3 Faction victory 7 Natural disaster 11 Ritual/curse/omen
4 Faction sabotage 8 Monster migration 12 Outsiders arrive (caravan/army/pilgrims)

D) One-sentence template

Over time, ____ (thing) ____ (how) because ____ (why).

OUTPUTS TO COLLECT (end of Session –1)

  • Tab 1: 10–15 truths + 3 strong “starting situation” facts
  • Tab 2: each player’s “want” + 2 fun dials + 1 boredom trigger
  • Tab 3: 3–6 threats/factions with “want / doing now / if ignored”
  • Tab 4: reward priorities + 6 town-pull reasons
  • Tab 5: 3–6 change statements using the template

Tip: have everyone write big/legible; you’ll transcribe into your master binder later.
