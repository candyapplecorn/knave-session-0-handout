DUNGEON SEED GENERATOR (~36 rooms; 2+ factions; classic exploration)

Roll d20 for ideas. Link results together. Empty rooms are pacing. Traps signal danger. Treasure equals XP.

1) ORIGIN & ATMOSPHERE

Quick Prompts

  • Original Purpose: ________
  • Current State: ________ (flooded, overgrown, burnt)
  • Dominant Smash/Odor: ________
  • Weird Lighting: ________

d12 Original Purpose

1 Tomb / Crypt 5 Mine / Quarry 9 Wizard's Tower (sunken)
2 Temple / Shrine 6 Prison / Oubliette 10 Sewers / Cistern
3 Fortress / Outpost 7 Natural Caverns 11 Laboratory / Archive
4 Vault / Treasury 8 Monster Lair 12 Portal / Gatehouse

d12 Distinctive Vibe

1 Dripping Wet / Moldy 5 Scorched / Ashy 9 Overgrown roots
2 Dust-choked / Dry 6 Freezing / Icy 10 Crystal resonant humid
3 Sticky / Web-filled 7 Unnatural silence 11 Mechanical hum
4 Smell of Rot/Sulfur 8 Howling wind 12 Whispers / Echoes
Concept: "Originally a ____, now claimed by ____. The air smells of ____."

4) HAZARDS & TRICKS

Telegraph traps! Click, snap, loose stone. Damage is boring; consequences are fun.

Quick Prompts

  • Common Trap Trigger: ________ (tripwire, plate)
  • Puzzle Reward: ________ (shortcut, loot)

d12 Trap Concept

1 Pit (Spiked/Water/Slime) 5 Falling Block/Net 9 Alarm (Summons Monster)
2 Poison Dart/Gas 6 Scything Blade 10 Teleport / Separation
3 Rolling Boulder 7 Room fills w/ Sand/Water 11 Curse (Stat drain)
4 Crushing Walls 8 Magical Rune (Explodes) 12 Illusion floor

d12 Trick / Puzzle Object

2 Chessboard Floor 6 Altar demanding blood 10 Musical instrument
3 Lever Array (colored) 7 Pool of liquid light 11 Portrait with eyes
